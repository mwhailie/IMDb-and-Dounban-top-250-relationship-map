Welcome to Movie Map

A study of popular movies in IMDb and douban


What is it?

Inspired by 低分电影宇宙, I built this site to show how IMDb top 250 movies and 豆瓣电影 top 250 are connected to each other by their actors and directors. Feel free to try:

  • Zoom in and out with your mouse
  • Hover over a node to show the name of the movie or persion
  • Click on movie nodes to show the poster, rank, director and navigate to the page
